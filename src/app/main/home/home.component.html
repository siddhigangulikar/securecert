ABOUT US

ONE-OF-A-KIND DEGREE VERIFICATION SYSTEM

Secure creation of your certificate

Issuing a certificate is relatively simple -- we create a digital file(JSON) that contains academic information such as the name of the recipient, the name of the Issuer (University), an issue date, etc.

Easier access to the student

Newly improved, The recipient (Student) can access the certificates through our web application and share the certificate with the concerned parties using the URL.!

Authentication & Verification

The Verifier (Employer) can verify to whom the certificate was issued to, by whom, and validate the content of the certificate itself and the owner of the certificate.
