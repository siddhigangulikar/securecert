ABOUT

Our aim was creating a software for creating, issuing, viewing and verifying blockchain-based certificates.

These records are registered on a Blockchain network, cryptographically signed and secured, these records are shareable allowing anyone to obtain instant access and confirmatation that the certificate is valid and up to date.
